Instant Noodles

An easy recipe anyone can make!

One of the most popular choices for comfort, easy to make and filling dishes is instant noodles. Made in a matter of minutes, they can be a real lifesaver.

We will be demonstrating how you can spice up the boring old instant noodle and enjoy it in a new flavour. Get ready to make the best noodles of your life!

Ingredients

  • Instant noodles pack (with seasoning)
  • Peas, carrots, garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Take enough water in a pot and bring it to a boil. Add the noodle cake and veggies in the water and let them cook for ~4 minutes.
  2. By the time the stuff in the pot cooks, heat up the oil in a pan and add crushed garlic to it along with the noodle seasoning. Cook it until the garlic turns golden brown.
  3. Strain the water to separate the noddles and vegetables. Put them in a bowl
  4. Now add the searing hot oil and seasoning on the boiled noodles and mix well. You are done! Enjoy your meal!
