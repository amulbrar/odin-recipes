~Banana Milkshake

Healthy, filling, and yummy!

What's better than a cold milkshake on a sunny day? And what's a better milkshake than banana milkshake?

So today we will be learning how to make a really tasty banana milkshake!

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Milk
  • Fruits (strawberries, blueberries, etc.)
  • Oreos

Steps

  1. Dice your fruits into little chunks and toss them in the mixer.
  2. Add milk and oreos to the mixer. Close the lid and mix it until you're satisfied with the consistency.
  3. And you're done! Take out the milkshake in a glass and enjoy! Add ice if you want it chilled!
